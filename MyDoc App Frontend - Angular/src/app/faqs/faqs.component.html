Read the following instructions carefully and follow them to schedule an appointment.

  • Create an account by signing up in the {{title}}.
  • If you already have an account, login with your respective username and password.
  • Go to my doctors tab.
  • Choose the doctor you want to book an appointment with.
  • Choose the suitable date and time slot which is available. Availability is decided on first come first serve basis.
  • Once, the slot is chosen, make payment to book the chosen slot. Appointment booking process is complete only on successful payment of token money.
  • Once booking is done, it will reflect in the profile of the user.
